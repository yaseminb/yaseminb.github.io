Research

My research focus is on building robotic systems that can achieve various tasks in real environments autonomously. I develop learning based approaches for robotic systems to perceive their environments and their status, to interact, e.g. through grasping and manipulation actions using multi-sensory data e.g., tactile, visual and proprioceptive, and to encode knowledge obtained from experience. My long-term research is focused on data driven learning that will allow robots to learn in a data efficient manner from multi-sensory data, making use of analytic approaches where possible having the best of both worlds.
